# Track Charter — AI Workplace

> **OU Lead:** Kim Vogel (CVP, AI Business Solutions) — Duane Mataczynski (GM, ABS Workplace AI)  
> **Americas SE&O:** Scott Prather

AI Workplace is the M365-led horizontal selling motion. SEs here demo productivity, collaboration, and frontline worker transformation using M365 Copilot, Teams, Copilot Chat, and the E5-E7 license stack.

---

## Outcome statement

> **AI Workplace SEs show customers that Microsoft 365 Copilot doesn't just assist individuals — it transforms how entire organizations communicate, decide, and act. Every knowledge worker and frontline employee becomes measurably more effective the day they turn it on.**

## Relationship with AI Business Process

AI Workplace and AI Business Process both sell M365 Copilot, but from different angles:

| | AI Business Process | AI Workplace |
|---|---|---|
| **Primary product** | D365 (CRM/ERP) | M365 (productivity/collaboration) |
| **Copilot angle** | Copilot *for D365* (Sales, Service, FS agents) | Copilot *in M365* (Copilot Chat, Teams, Word) |
| **Shared layer** | `../_shared/m365-copilot/` | `../_shared/m365-copilot/` |
| **Bridging leader** | Duane Mataczynski (GM, ABS Workplace AI) | same |

**Rule of thumb:** If the demo starts in a D365 app → AI BP. If it starts in Teams or M365 Copilot Chat → AI Workplace. Joint demos use both — coordinate via `_shared/m365-copilot/`.
