# Track Charter — Power Platform (Low Code)

> **OU:** AI Business Process  
> **Org classifier:** `AI Biz Sol - Power Plat` under Commercial Solution Areas  
> **OU Lead:** Kim Vogel (CVP, AI Business Solutions)  
> **Product alignment:** Jessica Kim (Solution Play Leader — Low Code, Agents) | Catherine Chambers (Power Apps + Power Automate) | Erol Atac (Copilot Studio)

Power Platform SEs own the *how* — custom app development, automation, Copilot Studio agents, and platform extensibility. They are the builder arm of AI Business Process, distinct from but tightly partnered with the D365 (Sales & Service) SE team.

---

## Outcome statement

> **Power Platform SEs prove that Microsoft’s low-code platform lets any organization — IT team or business maker — build the solutions they need in weeks, not months, with AI agents and automation that keep working long after the demo ends.**

---

## Role vs. neighboring SE roles

| Dimension | **Power Platform SE (this track)** | **Sales & Service SE (D365)** | **Copilot SE** |
|---|---|---|---|
| Primary lens | Platform / app dev / extensibility | Business process transformation (packaged CRM/ERP) | M365 Copilot + AI Workforce |
| Core products | Power Apps, Power Automate, Copilot Studio, Dataverse, Power Pages, AI Builder | D365 Sales, D365 Service, D365 FS | M365 Copilot, Copilot Chat |

## Core demo patterns

### The Citizen Developer Narrative
> *"Low code empowers the partnership between IT and the business. IT focuses on security, governance, compliance, and scale — while the business focuses on solving problems. Power Platform brings these skill sets together to turn ideas into solutions."*

**Key stat:** *"More than 65% of app development will be low-code."*

### Patterns of Value (4 anchor motions)
| Pattern | Description |
|---|---|
| **App Modernization** | Replace legacy/Excel/paper processes with Power Apps |
| **Business Process Improvement** | Automate approval chains, multi-system workflows with Power Automate |
| **Hyperautomation** | RPA + cloud flows + AI Builder for end-to-end automation |
| **Employee Experience** | Self-service tools, onboarding apps, knowledge agents in Teams |

### Governance Conversation Guide
3-phase flow (April FY26 PP SE Community Call):
1. **Assess** — governance discussion, pre-call questions, review current state
2. **Demonstrate** — Admin Center, Managed Environments, CoE Starter Kit
3. **Operationalize** — governance workshop, define model, identify hotspots

> *"Governance isn’t the brake pedal — it’s the launchpad."*

### Agent + Workflow Orchestration Pattern
```
Copilot Studio (agent) → Power Automate (workflow) → Connectors/APIs
"conversation → action → system update"
```

### RAPP Pattern
Capture business problem from customer transcript → rapid prototype → working demo video next day.

## Customer personas

| Persona | Who they are | What they care about |
|---|---|---|
| **Citizen Developer / Maker** | Business user building for their team | Speed, no IT bottleneck, approachable tooling |
| **Power User / Super User** | Champion driving adoption in a BU | Templates, reuse, governance guardrails |
| **IT Pro / Platform Admin** | Governs environments, DLP, licensing | Security, compliance, scale, cost control |
| **Enterprise Architect** | Designs cross-system integration | Platform vs. Azure vs. custom code decision |
| **CxO / BDM** | Signs the deal | ROI, risk reduction, speed to value |

### Maker maturity model
| Level | Description |
|---|---|
| **L100** | No pro dev; pure citizen dev |
| **L200** | Pilot with IT guardrails |
| **L300** | Shared ALM + reusable components |
| **L500** | Fully integrated fusion teams — shared goals, shared source control |
