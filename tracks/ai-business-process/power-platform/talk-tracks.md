# Power Platform SE — Talk Tracks & Objection Handling

> For use in any customer engagement where Power Platform / low-code is part of the conversation.

---

## Opening talk tracks

### The Iceberg (universal opener)
> *"The visible IT work — the apps IT builds and maintains — is just the tip of the iceberg. Below the surface is a massive backlog of unmet business demand: processes running on spreadsheets, manual approvals over email, tribal knowledge in people’s heads. Power Platform lets the business build solutions to those problems without waiting years for IT. And IT keeps governance, security, and the platform. That’s the partnership we’re here to talk about today."*

### For a Maker/Developer audience
> *"Whether you like no-code, low-code, or pro-code — you’re welcome here. Power Platform meets developers where they are. Citizen developers build canvas apps in an afternoon. Pro developers extend with custom connectors, PCF components, and Azure Functions. The platform scales with your ambition."*

### For a CxO / BDM
> *"Your competitors are not waiting for their IT backlog to clear before they modernize. The organizations pulling ahead are the ones where a business analyst can build a working app in a week. We’re going to show you what that looks like — and what it costs when you don’t have it."*

### For IT / Platform Admin
> *"Governance isn’t the brake pedal — it’s the launchpad. We’re going to show you how to give the business the freedom to build while keeping security, DLP, and compliance controls exactly where you need them."*

## Common objections

### "We already have ServiceNow / Salesforce / legacy tool"
> *"Great — Power Platform connects to those. We’re not asking you to rip anything out. We’re asking whether you want your people to be able to build on top of those systems without writing code. Power Automate has 1,000+ connectors. Your investment stays; you just get a layer your business can actually use."*

### "Low-code isn’t enterprise-grade"
> *"Dynamics 365 is built on Power Platform. It runs some of the largest manufacturing operations in the world. The same platform your citizen developers use is the same platform Microsoft runs its own CRM on. Enterprise-grade isn’t a question — it’s the foundation."*

### "We don’t have the skills"
> *"That’s exactly why low-code exists. We’re not asking you to hire developers. We’re asking you to find the people in your business who already solve problems with Excel — and give them a better tool. Most customers identify their first Power Users in the pilot. They tend to be the person everyone already goes to for help."*

### "We’re worried about shadow IT / ungoverned apps"
> *"The shadow IT you’re worried about is already happening — in spreadsheets, in personal SharePoint sites, in email chains. Power Platform with Managed Environments gives you visibility and control you don’t have today. We’re not creating the problem; we’re giving you the tools to manage it."*

### "We need to think about our AI strategy first"
> *"Power Platform is your AI strategy made practical. Copilot Studio lets you build agents that connect your data to AI without building models from scratch. You don’t need an AI strategy to start — you need a use case. Tell me one process in your business that still runs on email or spreadsheet, and we’ll show you what an agent looks like for that process today."*

---

## Copilot Studio positioning (vs. Azure OpenAI direct)

> *"If your developers want to build with Azure OpenAI directly, that’s great — that’s the Azure play. Copilot Studio is for when you want business users and IT to build agents together, with governance built in, deploying to Teams or D365 or your website without writing orchestration code. You build the agent once; it shows up where your people already work."*
