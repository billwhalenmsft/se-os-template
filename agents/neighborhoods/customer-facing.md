# 🤝 Customer-Facing Neighborhood

> The neighborhood that owns the customer-facing surface — narratives, demos, outcomes, and voice.

## Residents

| Persona | Role | Specialty |
|---|---|---|
| **Vera Holm** | Customer Persona Simulator | Channels the buyer's voice; sanity-checks every artifact |

| **Jonas Reid** | Content Strategist | Owns the words — narrative, structure, polish |

## What this neighborhood owns

- Customer-facing demo guides
- Customer-facing SOPs (pre-meeting, post-meeting)
- Use case docs and outcome statements
- Narrative arcs (five-beat demo flow)
- The customer "voice" in any artifact

## Default rhythm

- **Daily** — review the customer meeting queue; Vera scans for any artifact going out in the next 48 hours
- **Per artifact** — Priya signs technical accuracy; Vera signs customer voice; Jonas signs prose; Felix signs outcome framing
- **Weekly** — review what shipped; pull patterns into the shared library
