What's Inside

Three layers. One platform.

SE OS is not a chatbot. It's a full operating model — from a command-center UI to a Digital SE Team to deep technical specialists you can install in seconds.

🖥️

SE Command Center

A dark-themed dashboard that surfaces top priorities, action items, calendar, recent customers, and one-click workflows. Less cluttered than a chat window. Built for the SE that ships, not browses.

🧑‍💼

Digital SE Team

12 named AI personas with backstories, working styles, and quality bars — organized into 4 neighborhoods (Customer-Facing, Build-Time, Quality, Strategy). They partner with you, not replace you.

🧠

Specialist Cloud Agents

CLI plugins that go deep on one technical job. Power Platform analysis, D365 audits, Foundry config — the generalists call them; you ship customer-grade artifacts in 60 seconds.

The Reinvention

Five convictions we will not negotiate.

From the SE OS Manifesto — what we believe about what it means to be an SE at Microsoft, now and forever.

1

SEs are architects, not assemblers.

Stop hand-building one-off demos. Compose, customize, and curate from a living library of reusable assets.

2

Knowledge must compound.

Every customer conversation, every demo, every win — feeds back into the team's collective intelligence. No more re-learning.

3

Agents are colleagues, not chatbots.

They have names, backstories, working styles, and quality bars. You assign work to them like you would a junior consultant.

4

Customer time is the only time that matters.

Every minute the system saves is a minute returned to the customer conversation. The metric is hours-back-per-week.

5

Quality compounds — or trust collapses.

Three guilds (AI Craft, Demo Excellence, Customer Empathy) enforce review gates on everything that leaves the team.

The Measurable Promise

If it doesn't move the metric, it's not working.

Every SE OS deployment instruments the Time-Back Metric — a weekly tally of hours saved from SE overhead and returned to customer conversation. The reinvention is real or it isn't. The number says which.

⏱️

Hours-back per week

Tracks five overhead categories (prep, demo build, context hunting, follow-up, onboarding) against a per-SE baseline.

📊

Team aggregate view

Rolls up across an SE team so leaders can see deployment ROI. 100 SEs × 20 hrs/wk = 2,000 hours back.

🚦

Stall detection

If the number isn't moving after 4 weeks, Maya (Strategy) auto-flags it. The system tells you when it's not earning its keep.

Get Started

From zero to SE OS in under 10 minutes.

SE OS is a GitHub template repository. Click "Use this template" and you get your own copy — customize it for your practice area, your customers, your demos.

🚀 Stand up SE OS

1
Click Use this template on the repo. Name your fork. Make it private if you want.
2
Open it in VS Code: code your-se-os
3
Open web_ui/index.html in your browser — that's your Command Center.
4
Edit agents/personas/ to match your team. The personas are yours to evolve.

🧠 Add your first specialist

1
From any terminal: copilot plugin install promptclickrun/power-agents-blueprint
2
Drop a Power Platform solution ZIP into your repo's customers/<name>/
3
In a Copilot CLI session: analyze customers/<name>/solution.zip
4
Specialist produces a self-contained HTML dashboard. Share with the customer.
