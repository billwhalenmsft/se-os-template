How this is measured

The Time-Back Metric is the reinvention's measurable promise. It tracks hours returned from SE overhead (prep, asset assembly, context-gathering, follow-up admin) back to customer conversation time.

Data source: web_ui/time-back-data.json — updated weekly by Aria (Strategy neighborhood). Baseline is established during the first 2 weeks of SE OS adoption by asking the SE to honestly log their pre-SE OS time allocation.

What "counts" as time saved: only hours the SE would have otherwise spent on overhead work (prep, assembly, hunting context). Hours spent on better customer conversation, deeper technical work, or building reusable assets are reinvested, not "saved."

Goal: If the system is working, you should see weekly hours saved trend upward and stabilize at ~20+ hrs/week within 60 days. If it stays flat, that's a signal the SE is not actually leaning on the Digital SE Team — and Maya should investigate why.
