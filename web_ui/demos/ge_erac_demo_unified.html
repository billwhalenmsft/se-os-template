📖 The Demo Story — Monday Morning Cedent Review

Setting: You are Morgan, an Underwriting Director on the GE Verisk Reinsurance team. Every Monday morning your team performs a Partnership Review & Risk (PRR) review across the Tier 1 and Tier 2 cedents in your book. Today you have an hour before the leadership stand-up to triage the watchlist, validate reserve adequacy on two cedents flagged last week, and confirm renewal posture for two treaties closing in Q3.

The Challenge: Today, every one of those tasks lives in a different place. Cedent ratings are in Excel on a SharePoint site that 14 people share. Treaty exposure is in a second workbook updated nightly by an analyst. Reserve adequacy lives in a BI report that takes 8 minutes to load. Disputes are tracked in email threads. There is no single Cedent record — just a name on multiple spreadsheets.

  • Spot a watchlist cedent from the PRR dashboard and drill into their full story in one click
  • Use the Cedent 360 view to see tier, AM Best rating, GWP, renewal date, reserve adequacy, and open disputes — all in one frame
  • Pivot to the Treaties grid and run live charts of exposure by status — no BI report needed
  • Validate Reserve Adequacy trend on a flagged cedent
  • Open a Risk Assessment with approval status and a Dispute with live exposure tracking

The Differentiator vs Excel + BI: Everything you see today is in one Dataverse model-driven app, governed by Microsoft 365 security, ready for Copilot. No data wrangling. No version-of-truth conflict. The cedent record IS the source of truth.
