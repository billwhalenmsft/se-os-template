📖 The Demo Story — A Day at Navico Customer Care

Setting: You are Alex, a Customer Service Representative on the Simrad brand team at Navico's global support center. Navico currently uses Salesforce for Sales — today, you're showing how D365 Customer Service can serve as the expansion for Customer Service, replacing the basic case management they already do in D365 while adding omnichannel, intelligent routing, and Copilot.

The Challenge: Navico has 37 CSRs, 17 brands, and a customer base split between B2B distributors (Tier 1) and B2C end users. Today you'll handle five types of interactions:

  • A high-priority inbound call from a Platinum Expert B2B distributor
  • An inbound email about a technical product issue — intelligent routing + Copilot Agent Assist
  • A live web chat from a Platinum Expert B2B distributor via the dealer portal (a channel Navico doesn't have today)
  • An RMA warranty case that needs serial lookup and processing
  • A self-service AI agent on the portal — handling troubleshooting, warranty, registration, and certification without a human

The Differentiator vs Salesforce: Navico already owns the D365 license. What they're missing is configuration — channels, intelligent routing, Copilot Agent Assist, and the full omnichannel workspace. Everything you'll see today is available to them TODAY without additional license cost.
