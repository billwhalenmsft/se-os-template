🎭 The Story

You’re showing a Persona Workbench — a unified cockpit that adapts its view based on who’s looking at the same pipeline data. Each persona (Sales Rep, Manager, Coordinator, Marketing) sees exactly what they need.

The demo walks through a real morning workflow: Sarah (Sales) checks her pipeline, James (Manager) reviews approvals and exceptions, Mike (Coordinator) manages the quote queue, and Priya (Marketing) tracks campaign attribution.

Key message: One pipeline, one AI system, four tailored experiences — no context switching, no searching, no “let me look that up.”
