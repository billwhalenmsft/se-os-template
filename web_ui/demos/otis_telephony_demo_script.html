💬 Objection Handling

"We have customized USD heavily - can we keep our customizations?"

→ "Yes, the agent scripts and workflows you've built in USD can be migrated to Customer Service workspace. We can also enhance them with AI-powered suggestions."

"What about our existing Ring Central numbers?"

→ "You can port your existing numbers to Microsoft voice channels, or use Direct Routing to keep your existing carrier while getting the integrated experience."

"How does this work with Field Service?"

→ "That's one of the biggest advantages - it's all the same platform. When Emma creates a work order, it flows directly to Field Service scheduling. No integration needed."
