🎬 Hero Case Demo Script

1 Healthcare Entrapment Emergency

"I just got a call - there's someone trapped in an elevator at Riverside Medical Centre. It's Elevator 3, a bed elevator. The facilities manager David Chen called it in."
Expected: Agent recognizes entrapment emergency, references KB-001247 for procedure, notes healthcare SLA (1 hour), provides step-by-step guidance.
  • Notice how the agent prioritizes safety and immediate action
  • It references the correct KB article for entrapment procedures
  • Healthcare SLA is automatically considered

2 High-Rise Door Issue

"Imperial Tower on Deansgate is reporting door alignment problems on their SkyRise elevator. Doors are slow to close on floor 15."
Expected: Agent references KB-002154 for SkyRise door troubleshooting, checks contract status, suggests diagnostic steps.
  • Agent pulls relevant troubleshooting steps from knowledge base
  • Suggests remote diagnostic options
  • Considers safety implications

3 Contract/Billing Question

"Customer at MediaCity wants to know what's covered under their Silver contract. Are emergency calls included?"
Expected: Agent references KB-003106 for contract coverage guide, explains Silver tier benefits and limitations.
  • Agent accurately describes contract coverage
  • Can upsell to Gold if appropriate
  • References authoritative knowledge source

4 Modernization Opportunity

"Manchester Central Hotel is asking about upgrading their 25-year-old elevator. What modernization options do we have?"
Expected: Agent recognizes sales opportunity, provides general info, offers to warm transfer to modernization sales team.
  • Agent identifies this as a sales opportunity
  • Provides helpful context without overstepping
  • Smoothly transitions to sales handoff

5 IoT Alert Response

"We got an IoT anomaly alert for elevator at MediaCityUK. Sensor shows unusual vibration patterns."
Expected: Agent references KB-003145 for IoT monitoring interpretation, suggests proactive maintenance scheduling.
  • Proactive service before customer notices issue
  • Explains IoT alert significance
  • Recommends preventive action
